Who We Are

Designing with purpose, building with conscience.

Sthaapatya Studio is an architectural and environmental consultancy venturing into the green building and development market. With ongoing experience in architecture, interiors and environmental design, we operate from offices in Pune and Nashik.

We are passionate about the environment and strive to build a society which not only thinks Green but also lives Green. We deliver education and guidance to builders, developers, architects, designers, engineers, consultants, and contractors on green building practices, strategies, and implementation.

The Mind Behind

Sailee Raisoni Bafna

Director & Architect · Founder, Sthaapatya Studio

Sailee completed her Bachelor's degree from Allana College of Architecture (Pune University) in 2011. With post-graduation in Environmental Planning, she completed her Masters in 2013 from Dr. B.N. College of Architecture.

She has worked with prestigious architects in sustainable architecture, industrial, residential and commercial buildings. She also had the opportunity to work with the Dy. Chief Architect (2011) of Pune for the redesigning of Ahilyabai Holkar's birth place into a museum.

Her design philosophy hinges on ecological design, modular and incremental approaches, standardization, and flexible & temporal concepts in space design.

  • 2011 B.Arch — Allana College of Architecture, Pune University
  • 2013 M.Arch (Environmental Planning) — Dr. B.N. College of Architecture
  • 2020 Founded Sthaapatya Studio — Pune & Nashik
